About the Designer

“It always seems impossible until it’s done.” - Nelson Mandela

Nice to meet you, I'm Jonah Corker a Web Designer based in Cape Town, South Africa. After I left school I studied at False Bay College where I got a degree in Civil Engineering. I thought that I had everything worked out, soon I realized that I didn't enjoy what I was doing. I didnt want to be the person that says the oversued sentence, "we all do work that we don't enjoy doing".

That's when I decided to pursue my dream, instead of running after the prospect of making lots of money in the future. It was worthless to me if I didn't follow my dream of designing. So, everyday after I finished working, I practiced how to code and studied Graphic Design for at least 4 hours every day for a month. Thats when I decided to take the leap to quit my job and invest all the time I had into learning Web Design.

My love for Web Design could never fade, the way colors can make people feel joy, or how a small family business now has lots of customers because of a wesite you created for them. that's what makes me truly happy, seeing others happy and getting everything they deserve. I'm not someone who gives up easily, if I know what I want I'll work hard to get it. All I need now, is the oppotunity to put what I've learnt into practice with a real world work enviroment, so if we are on the same webpage please contact me using any method below.
